# ICAI Announces Applicability of Income-tax Act, 2025 for CA Exams from May 2027 Onwards

Ek important update hai. The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) ne announce kiya hai ki Income-tax Act, 2025 CA examinations mein May 2027 onwards applicable hoga. This is a significant change for all CA students preparing for their exams.

*   **New Act:** The Income-tax Act, 2025, which received Presidential assent on August 21, 2025, will replace the existing Income-tax Act, 1961. It is slated to come into effect from April 1, 2026, governing the tax year 2026-27.
*   **Applicability Date:** This new legislation will be applicable for CA examinations starting from May 2027.
*   **Affected Papers:**
    *   **CA Intermediate:** Paper 3A: Income-tax Law will now be examined under the provisions of the Income-tax Act, 2025.
    *   **CA Final:** Paper 4: Direct Tax Laws & International Taxation will also adopt the new Act for examinations from May 2027 onwards.
    *   **Self-Paced Online Modules:** Candidates taking Self-Paced Modules, specifically Set C - Paper 5: International Taxation, on or after May 1, 2027, will also need to study the updated Income-tax Act, 2025.
*   **Notification Details:** This announcement was made by ICAI on December 8, 2025.

Students are advised to take note of these changes to align their study plan with the revised syllabus applicability.
